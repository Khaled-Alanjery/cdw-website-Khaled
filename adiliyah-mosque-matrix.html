Procedural Model (grayscale) - click a zonenone selected
The mosque model is the darkest mass on the map, embedded as a real 3D layer directly in the map's own scene (MapLibre GL custom layer, one shared WebGL context) - not a separate overlay - sitting at its true geo-coordinate at every zoom/tilt level. Around it: pale spikes are real district topography (ASTER 30m, OpenTopoData), light-gray blocks are generic building massing over the real UNOSAT neighborhood footprints, and the neighborhoods themselves sink into voids below ground scaled to real % buildings damaged - loss shown as absence, not presence. Drag to orbit/tilt the whole scene · scroll to zoom · click a building part to select it. Minaret plan position is triangulated (see footer); its height (41m) and all dome/column/fountain geometry are measured from the CAD file or the damage report.
